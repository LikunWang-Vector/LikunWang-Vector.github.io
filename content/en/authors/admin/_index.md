## About Me

Likun Wang is an Algorithm Engineer at Shenzhen Suanhai Technology Co., Ltd., specializing in TPU operator development and toolchain optimization. He recently graduated with a Master's degree in Information, Production and Systems Engineering from Waseda University, where his research focused on biomechanical topology optimization and deep learning applications.

His interdisciplinary research combines finite element analysis, deep learning, and bio-inspired design to predict loading forces on fish vertebrae structures. With extensive experience in AI/ML development, computer vision (YOLO networks), and scientific computing, he has contributed to projects ranging from intelligent driver emotion recognition systems to sustainable development analysis.

Wang has demonstrated leadership through various roles including class monitor, lab administrator, and project manager across multiple internships at leading tech companies including iFLYTEK, Alibaba Entertainment, and Sichuan Huadi. His work has been recognized through prestigious awards including the MEXT Scholarship, WCSMO-16 presentation, and multiple competition honors.
