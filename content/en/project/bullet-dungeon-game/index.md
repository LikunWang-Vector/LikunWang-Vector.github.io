## Project Overview

"Bullet Dungeon" is a roguelike side-scrolling game that I led as the main Game Designer and Tester. The project earned the Silver Award at the prestigious Tencent Game Design Workshop, demonstrating excellence in game design and player experience.

## My Role & Responsibilities

### Lead Game Designer
- **Core Gameplay Design**: Designed fundamental game mechanics, progression systems, and player interactions
- **Level Design**: Created engaging and challenging level layouts with balanced difficulty curves
- **Weapon Systems**: Developed diverse weapon mechanics and combat systems
- **Enemy Design**: Designed enemy behaviors, AI patterns, and encounter scenarios

### Testing & Quality Assurance
- **Player Testing Coordination**: Organized multiple rounds of internal testing sessions
- **Feedback Integration**: Collected and analyzed player feedback to drive iterative improvements
- **Balance Optimization**: Fine-tuned game difficulty, progression, and reward systems
- **Bug Tracking**: Identified and documented gameplay issues for development team

### Performance Optimization
- **Multi-device Compatibility**: Ensured smooth gameplay across various device specifications
- **Performance Tuning**: Optimized game performance for consistent frame rates
- **User Experience**: Enhanced UI/UX based on player testing feedback

## Game Features

### Roguelike Elements
- **Procedural Generation**: Dynamic level layouts for high replayability
- **Permadeath System**: Strategic risk-reward gameplay mechanics
- **Random Loot**: Diverse weapon and item drops to encourage exploration

### Side-scrolling Action
- **Fluid Combat**: Responsive controls and satisfying combat mechanics
- **Progressive Difficulty**: Carefully balanced challenge progression
- **Visual Polish**: Engaging art style and smooth animations
