This post contains exercise class materials for the Compiler Principles final exam at UESTC (University of Electronic Science and Technology of China).

The exercises cover:
- Regular expressions and finite automata
- Context-free grammars
- LL(1) and LR parsing
- Syntax-directed translation
- Intermediate code generation
- Code optimization techniques

Note: The content is primarily in Chinese as these are course materials from a Chinese university.
