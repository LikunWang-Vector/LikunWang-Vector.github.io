## Introduction

Are you annoyed by WPS Office popup ads?

Do you want to use a clean, ad-free office software?

Today, I'll show you how to **solve annoying ads in 10 seconds!**

---

## Steps

### 1. Right-click WPS Office and Open File Location

![](/images/posts/10秒去除WPS-Office弹窗广告教程（2023.3.3/be6270254c80.png)

### 2. Open the Second Folder

![](/images/posts/10秒去除WPS-Office弹窗广告教程（2023.3.3/d494ac5a677c.png)

### 3. Enter the "office 6" Folder

![](/images/posts/10秒去除WPS-Office弹窗广告教程（2023.3.3/0b0b89f3a81a.png)

### 4. Find the File "ksomisc.exe"

![](/images/posts/10秒去除WPS-Office弹窗广告教程（2023.3.3/197f249e2630.png)

### 5. Right-click and Run as Administrator

(If installed on system drive, double-click to run; if on non-system drive, run as administrator)

![](/images/posts/10秒去除WPS-Office弹窗广告教程（2023.3.3/14c918f98f96.png)

### 6. Click "Advanced..."

![](/images/posts/10秒去除WPS-Office弹窗广告教程（2023.3.3/44c33a23cd81.png)

### 7. Follow the Settings in the Image

Uncheck all the advertising and promotion options.

![](/images/posts/10秒去除WPS-Office弹窗广告教程（2023.3.3/ebcf4f89ea16.png)

### 8. Click Exit to Complete Setup

![](/images/posts/10秒去除WPS-Office弹窗广告教程（2023.3.3/d9a3b7255964.png)

By following these steps, you can disable ads and get a **clean, green, popup-free, ad-free** WPS!
