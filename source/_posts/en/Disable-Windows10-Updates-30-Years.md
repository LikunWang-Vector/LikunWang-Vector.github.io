## Simple Three Steps, Easy Setup, Zero Code, Zero Risk

The method is actually very simple:

### Step 1: Manually Change Date and Time to Your Desired Disable Date

**Settings → Time & Language → Date & Time → Turn off "Set time automatically" → Manually set date and time**

(I set it to 2054.04.07)

![](/images/posts/0风险禁用Windows10更新30年！/9de923b07a9d.png)

### Step 2: Set "Pause Updates for 35 Days"

But now the "35 days later" is calculated based on the year 2054!

**Settings → Update & Security → Advanced Options → Pause Updates**

![](/images/posts/0风险禁用Windows10更新30年！/c3567dfc68a1.png)

### Step 3: Change the Time Back

![](/images/posts/0风险禁用Windows10更新30年！/b598616f9f58.png)

**After changing back, check and you'll find the update date hasn't changed!**

![](/images/posts/0风险禁用Windows10更新30年！/a856ad4cfa83.png)
